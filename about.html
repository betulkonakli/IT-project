Other Google products and services

  1. Android - The most widely used operating system for smartphones.
  2. Blogger - View and create your own blog.
  3. Chromebook - Laptop using the Google ChromeOS.
  4. ChromeOS - Operating system developed by Google for laptop and portable computers.
  5. Froogle - Search for products based on prices, location, type, etc.
  6. Gmail - Free online e-mail service with over 1gb of storage and the best Spam protection available.
  7. Google+ - Google social networking site.
  8. Google AdSense - A service that pays website publishers or blog developers to show advertisements on their site.
  9. Google AdWords - Service that enables users to pay to advertise on the Google search engine and other websites using Google AdSense.
  10. Google AdWords - Service that enables users to pay to advertise on the Google search engine and other websites using Google AdSense.
  11. Google Alerts - Create alert text messages sent to an e-mail address each day or as it happens of web searches, news searches, etc.
  12. Google Analytics - Google Analytics allows anyone with their own domain to monitor and create reports of visitors to their website.
  13. Google App Engine - A service that provides users the ability to create scalable web services that use Google's resources.
  14. Google Books - Another fantastic service from Google that contains hundreds of thousands of books that can be searched.
  15. Google Calendar - A way to organize your schedule, synchronize, and share events with your friends.
  16. Google Chrome - The most popular desktop Internet browser.
  17. Google Custom Search Engine (CSE) - Service that allows you to create your own custom Google Search engine.
  18. Google Docs - A fantastic free solution from Google that allows you to open Microsoft Office documents as well as share them with other users with Internet access.
  19. Google Fiber - A limited service available in some places in the United States that offers a Fiber connection to the Internet.

Google Easter eggs

Google also has several well known hidden Easter eggs throughout its services. Below is a list of these hidden gems.

  1. Google H4x0r - Display Google's search page and results in leet speak.
  2. Google Klingon - Display Google's search page and results in Klingon.
  3. Google Pig Latin - Display Google's search page and results in Pig Latin.
